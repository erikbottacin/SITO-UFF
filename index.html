ABOUT THE MOVIE

Release: 2006
Direction: Christopher Nolan

The movie follows the intense rivalry between two magicians during
the Victorian-era in London. As they strive to outdo each other's
magical feats, their competition escalates to dangerous extremes.
The film explores themes of obsession, sacrifice, and the blurred line
between illusion and reality, keeping the audience captivated with
a non-linear narrative, intricate plot twists, and stellar performances.
